We emerged to continue a family tradition and follow the spirit of motor boating. Mongird 25 is the first launch of the company. The boat is inspired by 30's-60's of the 20th century, when high gloss wood and stainless steel were popular. However, the full concept strives for something more than that. By developing an exposed carbon fiber boat with high-quality materials we are changing the status quo. Smooth shape, flush deck, yet aggressive hull line; a combination of elegance and performance – that’s what makes our motorboat a perfect weekender.

MONGIRD 25

We want to combine our know how with your desire.

MONGIRD YACHTS was founded by Krzysztof Mongird, a titled sailor and water sports enthusiast, who has represented his country on many world class regattas. Krzysztof follows the long family tradition. This passion goes three generations back. It all started with his grandfather, a graduate of the Maritime University, who specialized in mechanics and spent his whole life working in the maritime industry. A similar career path was initially chosen by his Krzysztof’s father, the founder of the MONGIRD company, who also graduated the Maritime University, and supervised the construction of El Bravo in 1991 – the grandest and most luxurious yacht in the world at that time. Years of experience, both in yacht construction and using them in prestigious sport competitions, extensive knowledge and moreover passion led to founding MONGIRD YACHTS.
